This getting started guide will introduce you to the CMS tool - and familiarize yourself with the user interface. For a full guide to working within the tool, see the editorial documentation.

Logging into Brightspot brings you directly to the Dashboard. From here you are provided with widgets to help find and create new content quickly. The widgets on offer can be rearranged on the page to suit your specific needs and/or preferences. Let's take a look at them:

Sitemap
View all your pages and filter by content type
Recent Activity
Recent edits and content creation
Work Streams
Manage simple tasks with work streams
Create new
Create new pages with one click
Bulk Uploader
Upload files in bulk. Drag and Drop.
Schedules
Keep track of content going live
